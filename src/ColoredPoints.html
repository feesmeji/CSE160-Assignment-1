CSE 160 Assignment 1: Painting

Author: Fernando Mejia

Description: Simple paint program that draws on the screen when you drag with your mouse.

Instructions:

1. Simply start drawing by dragging your mouse across the screen. For a colorful experience, start by selecting the color you would like to draw with by clicling "Green" or "Red".

2. Select the shape of your brush from the list of buttons "Point", "Triangle", or "Circle".

3. If you want to use colors other than green or red, use the "Red", "Green", and "Blue" color sliders to mix the base green and red colors.

4. If you are drawing with the "Circle" function, you may adjust the amount of segments of your circle brush with the "(Circles) Segment Count" slider.

5. Once you are done drawing, use the "Clear" button to erase your drawing.

Special: Click on the "MyDrawing" button for a surprise!

Please use a browser that supports "canvas"
